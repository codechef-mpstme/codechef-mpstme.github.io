About Us

What is Codechef MPSTME?
Established in the year 2020 as a student college chapter of Codechef, we aim to give the students the ability to learn the skills they’d need to succeed in the world where technology is pacing at an astronomical rate. Our mantra is to foster practice and learning in a fun way.
What we do
Since our inception, we have successfully organised many industrial and academic events which have helped the participants ace through the world of programming and get inspired by the best developers.
